Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first flight.

Yes — all SkyMappers pilots hold active FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificates and carry commercial drone liability insurance (minimum $1M per occurrence). We file LAANC authorizations for every controlled-airspace flight and maintain current waivers for night operations and beyond-visual-line-of-sight work where required. Certificates are available upon request before any engagement.
Standard turnaround is 24–48 hours from the time the flight is completed. Orthomosaics and basic inspection reports are typically available within 24 hours; complex deliverables such as full point clouds, 3D meshes, or detailed volumetric reports may take up to 48 hours. Rush delivery (same-day or next-morning) is available for an additional fee — just mention it when you request your quote.
Deliverables vary by service but typically include: geo-referenced GeoTIFF orthomosaics, LAS/LAZ point clouds, DSM/DTM elevation rasters, and high-resolution JPEG inspection imagery. Reports are provided as PDF. All spatial data is projected in NAD83 / State Plane or WGS84 UTM by default — custom projections available on request. Files are delivered via a secure, time-limited download link; we can also upload directly to your preferred cloud storage or GIS platform.
We're actively building out our agricultural service line, including multispectral NDVI mapping, irrigation stress monitoring, and variable-rate application (VRA) prescription exports. We expect a limited pilot program to open for select growers in the upcoming growing season. If you'd like to be notified when ag services launch — or if you want to participate in our pilot program — email us at hello@skymappers.com and we'll add you to the early-access list.
SkyMappers currently serves a regional area with no travel surcharge for sites within 75 miles of our base. Projects beyond that range are welcome — a modest travel fee applies for mobilization. For large-scale or multi-site contracts requiring extended stays, contact us directly and we'll build a custom scope. We're expanding our crew footprint, so don't hesitate to reach out even if you're further afield.
